Company Values

Company Description

Aim Studios is a game development company that specializes in creating high quality and entertaining video games using high quality facilities including an animation and a music studio. Alongside the creation process of games, Aim Studios provide opportunities to new and upcoming developers by providing internships and online workshops to those who are interested. With that in mind, Aim Studios consists mainly of developers, digital artists and music artists and 2 teams handling each of the two triple A games that fund the company.

Logo Description

Aim Studios’ logo consists of 2 general ideas. A.I.M with the letter “I” designed as a crosshair with the purpose of providing a “goal” and a flying bullet, with “Studios” in the middle, below it, in which case the company interprets it as “A bullet will only stop when it hits its target or if it fails”. In the circumstance that the bullet fails to hit its target, “Shoot another bullet until you no longer can”. Aim Studios believes in a “never give up” Ideology providing others an opportunity while still aiming and developing for the future.

Be Lazy but Efficient

In Aim Studios, employees are encouraged to work smarter than harder. Employees identify the most efficient, time-saving approach to complete tasks without increasing more work or difficulty

Be an Opportunist

Aim Studios employees are born strategists. In Aim Studios, employees are quick to spot and seize favorable circumstances quickly.

Let your skills talk

Creating a harmonious balance between consistency and proficiency, we encourage our employees to continuously develop and showcase their skills, creating a culture of growth and the production of high-quality games.
