Organizational Structure

Aim Studios employees are born strategists. In Aim Studios, employees are quick to spot and seize favorable circumstances quickly.

CEO

The president and founder of the company. The one who manages everything.

General Manager

Works together with the Project manager to report their findings to the CEO. His/Her main job is to focus and monitor the general staff and the other managers allowing flexibility within the company.

Project Manager

Works together with the General Manager to report their findings to the CEO. His/Her main focus is to maintain and keep track of company projects including the works of all managers.

General Staff

Consists of the general jobs not related to game development like the Receptionist, Janitors, Human Resources, Marketing, etc. They report to the General Manager only.
